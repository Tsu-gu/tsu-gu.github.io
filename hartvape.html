Hartvape review

Updated on: 30.7.2025

This is a bit different from my usual articles but I felt like writing it anyways.

Obligatory smoking is bad for you, this is not an endorsement of vaping/smoking/using addictive substances.

I decided to give Hartvape a try as they are an Estonian company and their flavours seemed interesting.

The flavours I ordered are: Peach Ice, Strawberry Cosmo and Safari Tabacco.

Unboxing

Since I got to smell a peach flavour before and didn't hate, I opened that one first. The manual informed me not to drink the vape's liquid and in case I do that or it touches my skin, I should call an emergency number. This thwarted my plans of using this in a drink mixed with JÃ¤germeister.

First impressions

My first impressions are that the vape is much easier to draw from than the ones I had before. There's almost no force required to start it, you just pull and it doesn't even make a sound.

The back of it is a glossy plastic with a blue LED underneath. Looks very stylish if you ask me. There's no battery indicator but I have never seen it on this type of vapes so that's okay. Just throwing that out there in case you are expecting one.

Taste of Peach Ice

It's subtle. You can faintly taste the peach but it's not very strong. I don't mind this as my previous experience taught me that making a bad tasting liquid is very easy. Especially if it's a made up flavour like Cotton Candy or something. Overall it feels smooth. No complaints.

The vapour doesn't feel too hot either. (I like that)

Nicotine

These come in 0mg and 20mg variants. I of course ordered the 20mg one and it works as expected. Not much to add here.

The store

I ordered this from a SvetNapojov and chose DPD as the delivery method. Zero complaints whatsoever. The package was here within 2 days and I got a soda as a gift since the total value of the order exceeded 50 euros.
